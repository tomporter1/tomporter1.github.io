Chemistry AR Lab

Simulating chemical reactions in AR

Unity C# AR Chemistry

Project Overview

Made as part of my placement at Gaia Technologies, this app was a collection of chemistry mini-games that included the AR lab that simulated the reactions between different elements showing how the atoms are structured before and after the reaction.

The app was designed to be used in schools to help students understand the concepts of chemistry in a more interactive way. The AR Lab provided a sandbox environment where students could experiment with different elements and see the results of their reactions in real-time.

Technical Details

Key Features

  • Physical marker tracking
  • Virtual buttons to change the quantity of each element
