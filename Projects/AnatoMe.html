AnatoMe

AR experience to teach head muscle locations and their origin and insertion points

Unity C# AR

Project Overview

The application uses advanced AR tracking to position and anchor a detailed 3D skull model over the user's head. This mapping maintains precise positioning even as the user moves, providing a stable foundation for muscle placement learning. The tracking system accommodates different head sizes and shapes while maintaining anatomical accuracy.

Users progressively build their understanding by adding individual muscles to the skull model. Each muscle is rendered with accurate colouring and texture, allowing students to visualize the layered structure of facial musculature. The system provides immediate feedback on placement accuracy, helping users develop a thorough understanding of spatial relationships between different muscle groups.
